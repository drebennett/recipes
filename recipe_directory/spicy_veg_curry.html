Spicy Vegan Curry

Description:

A delicious vegan curry. Uses many vegetables and flavorful spices. Good with rice, or alone, like soup.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • One large yellow/white onion
  • Petit pois
  • Chickpeas
  • Canned chopped tomatoes
  • Many spices

Steps

  1. Peel potatoes and cut in bite sized pieces. Boil until just underdone.
  2. Chop onion and garlic. Add to pan with coconut oil.
  3. Add spices
  4. Add everything else
  5. Enjoy!
